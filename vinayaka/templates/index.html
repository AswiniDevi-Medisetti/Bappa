📖 The Divine Story of Vinayaka

Birth of Ganesha

Goddess Gowri created Ganesha by making an idol of her desire and gave life to the idol of turmeric and sandal, then Maa Gowri called Ganesha as her son!

The Guardian

Maa Gowri called Ganesha to guard and not allow anyone inside while she bathed. When Shiva came, Ganesha wouldn't allow him entry!

Shiva's Anger

Shiva, unaware Ganesha was his son, grew angry when denied entry. In his rage, he cut off Ganesha's head with his trident.

Parvati's Grief

Maa Gowri emerged to find her son dead. Overwhelmed with grief, she assumed a demonic form and began destroying nature.

Rebirth

Understanding the crisis, Shiva ordered Nandi to bring an elephant head from the north. Shiva attached it to Ganesha and restored his life.

The Golden Mango

Ganesha and Subramanya quarreled over a golden mango. Shiva declared whoever circled the world first would win the mango.

Divine Wisdom

While Subramanya circled the world, Ganesha wisely circled his parents, declaring them his world. Shiva declared Ganesha the winner.

Scribe of Wisdom

Veda Vyasa sought a writer for the Mahabharata. Shiva sent Ganesha, who wrote the epic as Vyasa dictated.

Moon's Laughter

When Ganesha fell from his mouse, the moon laughed. Ganesha cursed the moon to lose its shine and disappear periodically.

Divine Blessings

May Lord Ganesha bless your life with happiness, prosperity, and success. Stay safe, stay happy! 🕉️
